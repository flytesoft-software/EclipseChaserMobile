Eclipse Chaser Mobile App Changelog


Pre-release (09-02-104)

  • Fixed: Simulation will now properly show “No Eclipse” when no eclipse occurs.

  • Fixed: Stop multiple “Manual Location” warning dialogs from appearing when there is no GPS or internet connection.

  • Improved: Version information moved up and emboldened in “About” section.

  • Added: Changelog information.

v2.0.0 Beta 1 (10-02-2014)

  • Improved: Sun's simulated position derived using VSOP87D theory.

  • Improved: Moon's simulated position derived using ELP2000-82B theory.

  • Improved: Moon's simulated position RA inverted to reflect what a person see's in the sky.

  • Improved: (Ant)Umbral shadow drawing speed improvements.

  • Improved: (Ant)Umbral shadow now much more accurate near sunset and sunrise.

  • Improved: (Ant)Umbral shadow path uses black lines.

  • Improved: Selected Eclipse text now white for easier reading.

  • Update: jQueryMobile v1.4.4


Known Issues

  • Central eclipse lines do not go through until sunset.

  • Central eclipse lines do not have a sunset and sunrise limit line

  • South and north view limit lines do not merge well with east west limit lines.

  • Hybrid eclipse shadows do not properly phase from annular to total.

  • Hybrid eclipse central lines may not show up correctly throughout their full length.

  • Simulation of eclipse not yet accurate enough.

  • Lunar limb corrections not yet accounted for.

  • Atmospheric refraction not yet accounted for.
